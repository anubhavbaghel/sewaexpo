Frequently Asked Questions

When is SEWA Expo 2026?

SEWA Expo 2026 will be held on 12-13 September, 2026 at Park Plaza, Central Business District, Behind Karkarduma Court, East Delhi.

Is there an entry fee for visitors?

Registration is free for visitors. You can register online or at the venue on the event days.

How can I participate as an exhibitor?

Visit the "Exhibitor Information" page or contact us directly for exhibitor packages and details.

What are the parking arrangements?

Ample parking facilities, along with valet parking, are available at Park Plaza. Further details will be shared with registered participants.
