Celebrating Seenagers: Senior Teenagers

The 50+ generation represents a vibrant, active demographic redefining what it means to age. They're travelers, entrepreneurs, wellness enthusiasts, and community leaders.

SEWA Expo bridges exhibitors with this lucrative, engaged market—providing solutions for health, wealth, leisure, and meaningful living in the golden years.

Through interactive experiences, expert sessions, and community connections, we celebrate the vitality of India's Seenagers.
