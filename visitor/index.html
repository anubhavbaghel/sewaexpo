Who Should Attend?

SEWA Expo 2026 welcomes diverse audiences seeking innovative solutions for active aging and senior wellness

Primary Visitors

Active seniors ("seenagers") seeking wellness, fitness, and lifestyle solutions. Family members and caregivers supporting older adults. Health & wellness enthusiasts interested in preventive care and longevity.

Professional Visitors

Healthcare professionals: doctors, physiotherapists, dieticians, wellness consultants. Senior living and eldercare service providers. Financial planners, insurance advisors, and legal consultants specializing in retirement planning.

Lifestyle & Leisure Visitors

Travel and leisure enthusiasts aged 50+. Hobbyists and recreational activity seekers. Community and social group members exploring engagement opportunities.

Business & Institutional Visitors

Senior-focused product and service providers. Entrepreneurs and startups innovating for senior wellness. NGOs, government agencies, and social initiatives promoting senior enrichment.
